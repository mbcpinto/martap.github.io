About Me


Hey there, I'm Marta! I have a background in Biomedical Engineering, but my passion for creative expression has always been a big part of who I am. While pursuing my PhD in medical imaging, I discovered my interest in visual communication and the power of effectively communicating complex concepts to both experts and non-experts in the field. This led me to the exciting world of Data Analytics, which involves exploring data and extracting valuable insights. However, what truly fascinates me is the importance of Data Visualization in making those insights easily understandable and impactful.
I'm relatively new to the field of data visualization, but I am dedicated to learning everything I can about it. This blog is where I document my journey and share what I learn along the way. So if you're curious about data visualization and want to see how I'm working to enter this field, I invite you to join me on this exciting adventure!
